The project is a semester assignment in an OS class, with the goal of building a robot capable of playing a hybrid game of tennis/volleyball/slaughterhouse, on this field:

The details of the game can be read here, but the tl;dr version is as follows:

  • The robot with the highest score at the of the round wins.
  • Rounds last for 4 minutes
  • You score 2 points throwing a ball over the net without it bouncing out on the other side
  • You lose 1 point every time you touch the net
  • The robot must fit within a 40x40cm square at the beginning of a round, but may grow or deploy parts after the round has started
  • The robot must be completely autonomous

There will be a final tournament sometime in February, in two parts: A 1vs1 and a 2vs2 part (!).
